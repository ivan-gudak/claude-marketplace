# Dynatrace UI interaction terms

Correct verbs for describing user interactions with the Dynatrace web UI.
Source: styleguide.dynatrace.com/docs/style/ui-interactions

---

## Select (default for UI interaction)

"Select" is the device-agnostic equivalent of "click" and "tap." Use it for:
hyperlinks, option buttons, checkboxes, list values, menu items, keyboard keys,
text, code snippets, Hub apps.

- ✅ Select **Save**.
- ❌ Click **Save**.
- ❌ Tap **Save**.
- ✅ Edit the name and then select **Save**.

Only use "click" / "double-click" when the user is definitely using a mouse.
Only use "tap" when the user is definitely on a touchscreen.

---

## Go to (navigation)

Use "go to" for navigation to websites, file paths, tabs, and top-level menu items.

- ✅ Go to the **Operating systems** tab.
- ✅ Go to `dynatrace.com`.
- ✅ In Dynatrace, go to **Settings** and select **Dashboards** > **General settings**.
- ❌ Navigate to the **Settings** page.
- ❌ Open the **Operating Systems** tab.
- ❌ Locate the **Settings** page.

### "See" / "see also" for references
- ✅ For complete details, see <page title>.
- ❌ For complete details, go to <page title>.

### Greater-than symbol (>) for navigation sequences
- ✅ Go to **Settings** > **Preferences** > **Management zones**.
- Bold each UI location; don't bold the > symbols.
- Only for consistent navigation steps — not mixed actions.

---

## Open (display)

Use for desktop/mobile apps, dashboards, files, folders, browser tabs/windows,
terminal windows.

- ✅ Open the `config` file.
- ✅ Open a new terminal window.
- ❌ Open the **Operating systems** tab. (use "go to")
- ❌ Open the webpage. (use "go to")
- ❌ Launch Excel. (use "open")

---

## Close

For apps, dialogs, files, folders, notifications, alerts, tabs.

- ✅ Save and close the file.
- ❌ Exit the dialog.
- ❌ Quit the application.

---

## Clear

For removing items (cache, cookies), deselecting checkboxes, deleting folder
contents, creating empty states.

- ✅ Clear your browser's cache.
- ✅ Select or clear checkboxes as needed.

---

## Sign in (not "log in")

- ✅ Sign in to your Dynatrace account.
- ❌ Sign into your account. (wrong preposition)
- ❌ Log in to your account.
- "login" as a noun is acceptable.

---

## Turn on / turn off (not "enable" / "disable" / "toggle")

- ✅ Turn on **Automatic updates**.
- ✅ Turn off **Monitoring** for the selected entity.
- ❌ Enable **Automatic updates**.
- ❌ Switch off **Automatic updates**.
- ❌ Toggle the **Automatic updates** switch.

---

## Enter (not "type" / "paste")

- ✅ Enter your query in the **Search** field.
- ✅ Enter your access key.
- ❌ Type your query.
- ❌ Paste in your access key.

---

## Choose

Use for explicit choices or preference-setting from available options.

- ✅ Choose the right performance metric based on your goals.

---

## Move / drag

- "move" for repositioning (automatic or manual).
- "drag" for mouse-driven repositioning.

---

## Expand / collapse

- ✅ Expand the **Advanced settings** section.
- ❌ Open the **Advanced settings** section.

---

## Button references

Refer to the control label, not "button":
- ✅ Select **Install now**.
- ❌ Select the **Install now** button.

## Checkbox references

Use "select" and "clear," not "check/uncheck" or "mark/unmark":
- ✅ Select **Restart**.
- ✅ Clear **Restart**.
- ❌ Check **Restart**.
- ❌ Uncheck the **Restart** checkbox.
